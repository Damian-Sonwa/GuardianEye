## üé® Design System Summary

### Colors
- **Primary:** Emerald 600 (#059669)
- **Neutrals:** Slate 900, 700, 200, Zinc 50, 900
- **Alerts:** Red 600, Amber 500, Sky 500
- **Accents:** Indigo 600, Orange 600, Violet 500

### Typography
- **Font:** Inter
- **Scale:** 32px (H1) ‚Üí 13px (Small)
- **Weights:** Medium (500), Semibold (600), Bold (700)

### Spacing
- **Base:** 4px
- **Standard:** 16px, 24px, 32px
- **Padding:** 24px (cards), 16px (buttons)

### Components
- **Radius:** 12-16px (standard), 20px (large)
- **Shadows:** Soft, subtle (0 2px 8px)
- **Buttons:** 56px height (mobile), 48px (desktop)
